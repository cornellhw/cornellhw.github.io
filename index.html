I am a final-year master's student in the Dyson School Applied Economics and Management , Cornell University.

I graduated with a Master's degree from the Computer Science Department at the Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences at New York University in August 2022, preceded by a Bachelor of Arts in Economics and a Bachelor of Science in Electronic Information Engineering from Beijing Institute of Technology in June 2020.

My research interests include Food and Agricultural Economics, Environmental Economics, Development Economics, and machine learning applications.
